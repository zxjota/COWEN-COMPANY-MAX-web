This Privacy Policy explains how the NOXCORES Password Generator application handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the highest standards of data security.

Important: NOXCORES is an offline application that operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.

1. Introduction

Welcome to NOXCORES Password Generator ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding information when you use our password generator application.

NOXCORES is designed with privacy as a core principle. Unlike many applications, we are committed to a zero-data-collection approach. This means we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data from your device.

Offline-First Design

NOXCORES is built to function completely offline. All password generation, strength assessment, and storage occur locally on your device. No internet connection is required for core functionality, and no data leaves your device without your explicit permission.

2. No Data Collection Policy

NOXCORES adheres to a strict no-data-collection policy. Specifically:

What We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any identifying information
  • Generated Passwords: Passwords created by the app remain exclusively on your device
  • Usage Data: We do not track how you use the application, which features you access, or your preferences
  • Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Analytics: We do not use analytics or tracking tools that monitor your behavior

Application Functionality:

All functionality of NOXCORES operates entirely locally on your device:

  • Password generation algorithms run locally
  • Strength assessment and entropy calculations are performed on-device
  • Template storage and password history are saved only to your device's local storage
  • No external servers or cloud services are used for core functionality

4. Generated Passwords

Your generated passwords are handled with the utmost security:

Password Generation

Passwords are generated using cryptographically secure random number generators implemented locally on your device. The generation process includes:

  • Cryptographically secure randomness sources
  • Configurable character sets (uppercase, lowercase, numbers, symbols)
  • Exclusion of ambiguous characters (optional)
  • Real-time strength and entropy assessment

Password Storage

If you choose to save passwords within the app:

  • Passwords are stored only in your device's local storage
  • No encryption is applied (as the data never leaves your device)
  • You can delete saved passwords at any time
  • We recommend not storing highly sensitive passwords in the app history

Copy to Clipboard

When you use the "Copy to Clipboard" feature, the password is transferred to your device's clipboard. This is a standard operating system function, and we have no control over how other applications might access the clipboard. We recommend clearing your clipboard after pasting sensitive passwords.

5. Application Permissions

NOXCORES requests minimal permissions to function:

Required Permissions

  • Clipboard Access: Required for the "Copy to Clipboard" functionality to copy generated passwords
  • Local Storage: Required to save your preferences, templates, and password history locally on your device

Permissions We Do NOT Request

  • Internet Access: Not required for app functionality
  • Location Services: Not requested or used
  • Device Identifiers: Not accessed or collected
  • Contacts/Photos/Files: Not accessed unless you explicitly use export/import features

6. Security Practices

While NOXCORES operates entirely offline, we implement security best practices:

Code Security

The application is built following secure coding practices:

  • Use of cryptographically secure random number generation
  • No external dependencies on untrusted libraries
  • Regular code reviews for security vulnerabilities

User Security Recommendations

To maximize your security when using password generators:

  • Use the "Exclude ambiguous characters" option to avoid confusing characters
  • Generate passwords with sufficient length and complexity for your needs
  • Regularly clear your password history if storing sensitive passwords
  • Be mindful of clipboard usage when copying passwords
  • Consider using a password manager for storing important passwords
